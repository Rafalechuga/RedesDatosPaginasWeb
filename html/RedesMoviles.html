    • WIFI 6

    WIFI 6 Is a new brand technologie which promise revolutionate performance in different areas. The main points of improvement are:

  • Better capability of multi device support.
  • Speed ​​of 9.6Gbps against 3.5 of its predecessor.
  • It incorporates technology for latency reduction known as OFDMA (Orthogonal frequency division multiple access).
  • Incorporates MU-MIMO (Multiple User- Multiple Input Multiple Output) technology in which each device has direct communication with the router, unlike the previous generation in which a time segment was given for each device.
  • It also has the ability to send a more focused signal to the connected device.
  • Has a new security protocol, WPA3.
  • Increases the battery life of the devices by implementing TWT (Target wake time) by agreeing on a specific time to receive or send information.
  • Apart from the already known 2.5GHz and 5GHz band, the 6GHz band will also be implemented.
